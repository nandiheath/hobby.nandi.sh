In miniature painting, edge highlighting is one of the best techniques to enhance detail and depth.

### Core Techniques
1. **Water Control**: Keep your brush from being too wet.
2. **Use the Belly**: Use the side of the brush rather than the tip.
3. **Layering**: Start with a darker highlight and finish with the brightest color on the corners.
